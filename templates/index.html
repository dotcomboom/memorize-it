Memorize It!

This tool will help you quickly create memorization aids for lines, poems and other recitations.

Type what you need to memorize into a Word document, and Bold or Italicize text you would like to keep visible. Cues, headings, etc.


Your downloaded document will consist of:

  1. Your original text
    • "German Shepherd"
  2. A version of your text in a 'fill in the blank' format, where you can see each word's length but no more than the first letter
    • "G_____ S_______"
  3. A version of your text with only the first letter of each word
    • "G S"

Print out these documents with Word and cross-reference them as you read. Make it a goal to be able to read off of the First Letter variant alone, and then, completely from memory.
